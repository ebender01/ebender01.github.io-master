The library offers 20 computers with chargers to be checked out over night and over the weekend.
Students MUST fill out a paper checkout form agreeing to the following rules.


Conditions
  • The borrower is responsible for the equipment checked-out and listed below. All equipment will be inspected before check-out and upon check-in.
  • Laptop equipment is available on a first-come, first-serve basis.
  • The maximum checkout period is 24 hours (1 day). Checkout for a longer period of time must have approval from administration.
  • Please be prompt in your return of the equipment as others may be waiting to use it.
  • Download all of your data before check-in.
  • Failure to abide by these conditions will result in loss of laptop privileges and/or fines associated with the refusal to return equipment equaling the cost of the laptop.
  • All computers are due by the first bell at 8:25 am the next day. If you have not checked in or returned the computer by this time then the following consequences will take place.

                        1 First offense: NO computer checkout for 1 week.
                        2. Second offense: NO computer checkout for 2 weeks.
                        3. Third offense: NO computer checkout for a month.
                        4 Fourth offense: NO computer checkout for the rest of the semester.



Please see Ms. Bender in the library if you have any questions.
