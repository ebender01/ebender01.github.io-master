Library Use
All students, with a pass from a teacher, are welcomed to the library during school hours. Students must show their library pass to media center representative, and sign in at the circulation desk.

Lunch
The media center is open during on Tuesday and Thursday.
Students may come to the library during lunch. Computers may not be taken out of the library during lunch time.

Book and Equipment Check out
Students may check out up to five books for two weeks.
Books may be renewed twice.

Computer Use
Computers are provided for school use in the media center. Classes have priority for computer use, but individual students can use computers if they are available.
Computers may be checked out at the end of the day for overnight use.
Printers are available for student printing.
